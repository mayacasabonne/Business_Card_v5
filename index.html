How it works

You answer three set questions and one you choose yourself. Each question appears on screen with its own countdown. Recording runs straight through — no editing, no retakes mid-way. If you're not happy with it, record again from the start.

Recording is landscape and full screen. You'll pick your camera and microphone on the next-but-one step, then allow access when your browser asks.

Turn your phone sideways when the camera opens — recordings are landscape. Allow camera and microphone access when asked, and find a quiet spot with light on your face.
